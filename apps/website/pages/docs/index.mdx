# Sentry

import { Cards, Card } from '../../components/Card.tsx';

**Sentry** is a discord bot designed to make moderation simple again.

We focus on having a simple interface, with powerful features. We are constantly updating and adapting, with our open source development helping to improve and refine the quality of Sentry, and its associated services.

We have already developed first-class support for

- Attachment Scanning through [ClamAV](https://www.clamav.net/)
- Scanning domains against our compiled list of domains (named internally as "phishertools")

and we are continuously developing more features, that you can view and track the progress of on our [roadmap](/docs/roadmap).
